Join us for a fun-filled Easter celebration!

CBA Rabbitry is thrilled to announce we are going to partner with the nonprofit organization "Seattle Little Rangers" to bring you a FREE bunny petting experience at the Easter Egg Hunting event!

We’ll be showcasing our beautiful ARBA show-quality Netherland Dwarf bunnies, along with our friendly pet-quality bunnies for kids and families to meet, pet, and enjoy.

Check out the event poster for location and time details. See you there!
