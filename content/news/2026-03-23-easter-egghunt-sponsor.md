We are excited to announce that CBA Rabbitry is a proud sponsor of the 2026 Candy-Free Easter Egg Hunt!

In the spirit of keeping the holiday joyful and wholesome, this year's event is going candy-free — and we couldn't think of a better way to celebrate than by giving one lucky family the gift of a bunny.

**How it works:** At the event, we will be collecting adoption requests from families interested in welcoming a Netherland Dwarf into their home. Our rabbitry owner will personally review every request, and one deserving family will be selected to receive a show-line Netherland Dwarf baby from CBA Rabbitry — completely free.

Our show-line Netherland Dwarfs are ARBA-quality bunnies bred for beautiful conformation, sweet temperament, and a lifetime of companionship. This is a rare opportunity to bring home a truly special bunny.

Come find us at the event, meet our bunnies, and submit your adoption request. We look forward to finding the perfect home!
