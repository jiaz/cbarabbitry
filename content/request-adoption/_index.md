## Request an Adoption

Interested in bringing home one of our bunnies? Fill out the form below and we'll get back to you within 24–48 hours.

Please tell us which bunny you're interested in (or describe what you're looking for), and any questions you have. We want to make sure every adoption is the right match for both the bunny and your family.

**Already decided?** After we connect, you can place a deposit to officially reserve your bunny.

Not sure yet? Check out our [available bunnies](/bunnies-for-adoption/) or [join our waiting list](/join-waiting-list/) for upcoming litters.
