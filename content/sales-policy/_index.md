## Sales Policy

Please read our sales policy carefully before proceeding with an adoption. By placing a deposit or completing a purchase, you agree to these terms.

### Deposit

- A **non-refundable deposit** is required to reserve a bunny
- The deposit amount is applied toward the total purchase price
- If you change your mind or are unable to complete the adoption, the deposit is forfeited

### Health Guarantee

- All bunnies come with a **180-day genetic health guarantee**
- The guarantee covers **genetic conditions only** — not illness acquired after pickup
- An optional extended health plan is available for **$50**
- Bunnies must be taken to a veterinarian within 7 days of pickup to activate the guarantee

### Minimum Age

- Bunnies are **not released before 8 weeks of age**
- This ensures proper weaning, socialization, and health

### Breeding Restriction

- Our bunnies are sold as **pets only** unless explicitly agreed upon in writing
- Unauthorized breeding is subject to **$3,000 in damages**
- If you are interested in purchasing a bunny for breeding, please discuss this with us before purchase

### Payment

We accept the following payment methods:

- **Venmo**
- **Zelle**
- **PayPal**

Payment in full (or deposit) is required before or at time of pickup. We do not hold bunnies without a deposit.

### Questions?

If you have any questions about our policies, please [contact us](/contact/) before placing a deposit.
